# USF Risk Management Coursework Portfolio

Applied risk management coursework completed as part of RMI 3004: 
Principles of Risk Management at the University of South Florida 
(Spring 2025).

This repository demonstrates practical experience in risk identification, 
risk classification, likelihood and impact assessment, risk register 
development, risk matrix construction, and industry-level risk profile 
analysis — core competencies aligned with GRC (Governance, Risk, and 
Compliance) professional practice.

---

## Portfolio Contents

### Module 1 – Engagement Risk Register
- [Engagement Risk Register — Lululemon SEC 10-K Analysis](engagement-risk-register.md)

Risk register developed from analysis of a publicly traded company's 
annual SEC filing. Covers risk identification, pure vs. speculative 
classification, quadrant assignment, likelihood/impact scoring, and 
risk matrix visualization.

### Module 2 – Industry Risk Profile Analysis
- [Industry Risk Profile Analysis — CrowdStrike & Snowflake](industry-risk-profile-analysis.md)

Comparative risk profile analysis of two technology companies operating 
in the cybersecurity and cloud data analytics sectors. Covers strategic, 
operational, financial, and regulatory risk identification, and 
evaluation of each organization's risk management approach and 
mitigation strategies.
